# Portfolio Copy

## Short card description
Segmented a synthetic B2B customer portfolio using revenue, margin, payment behavior, and exposure metrics, then created risk-scored, BI-ready outputs for management reporting.

## Long project description
Customer Segmentation & Risk Evaluation is a synthetic portfolio case study that demonstrates how B2B customer master data can be transformed into actionable portfolio insights. The project builds a reproducible analytics workflow that generates synthetic customer records, engineers business-friendly features, applies segment clustering, and calculates an explainable credit risk score.

The final output includes customer-level risk categories, segment-level summaries, and a flat dashboard-ready dataset for Power BI. The interactive Streamlit demo provides executive and operational views, including segment performance, risk exposure, revenue and margin analysis, and customer drilldown capabilities.

The project is designed to showcase analytics engineering, business intelligence, risk analytics, and portfolio thinking without implying real company data or real business impact.
